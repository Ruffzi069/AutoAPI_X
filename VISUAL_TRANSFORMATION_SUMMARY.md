## 🎨 Design Philosophy Shift

### Old Philosophy (AI-Generated)
- **Flashy effects** - gradients everywhere
- **Purple overload** - brand color on everything

- **Consumer-focused** - mobile app vibes
- **Generic** - could be any dashboard

- **Subtle precision** - effects only when meaningful
- **Strategic color** - purple only for active states
- **Functional** - information over decoration
